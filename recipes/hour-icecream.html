Ice Cream

This is a homemade icecream that serves two people that is east to make

It should take no more than 60 minutes to make and prepare.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add 500ml of milk, 40g sugar, 12 icecubes, and 3 drops vanilla extract to blender
  2. Blend on high speed until thick
