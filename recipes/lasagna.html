Lasagna

This is a homemade lasagna that serves two people that is east to make

It should take no more than 30 minutes to make and prepare

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Pasta sheets
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cooke Mince for 5 mins
  2. Add Sauce and stir
  3. Boil pasta sheets for 3 mins
  4. Layer in a cooking dish mince, cheese, and pasta in that order till the dish is full
